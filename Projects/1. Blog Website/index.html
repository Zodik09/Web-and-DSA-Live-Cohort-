My Second Blog Post

In this post, I want to give you some of my Favourite Anime you should never miss it.


Here is my Favourite Animes' List:

  1. Shonen (Action, Adventure, Superpowers, Battles):
    • Naruto ‐ Ninjas, chakra-based abilities, and epic battles.
    • Dragon Ball Z ‐ Martial arts, super Saiyan transformations, and high-energy fights.
    • Mob Psycho 100 ‐ Psychic battles, humor, and character development.
    • Jujutsu Kaisen ‐ Cursed spirits, powerful sorcerers, and dark action.

  2. Psychological Thriller / Mystery:
    • Death Note ‐ Cat-and-mouse mind games, justice vs. evil, and supernatural elements.
    • Summer Time Rendering ‐ Time loops, mystery, and suspenseful storytelling.
    • Erased (Boku dake ga Inai Machi) ‐ Time travel, mystery, and emotional storytelling.

  3. Dark Fantasy / Post-Apocalyptic:
    • Attack on Titan ‐ Humanity vs. Titans, brutal survival, and deep lore.
    • Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) ‐ Demon hunting, sword fights, and breathtaking animation.

  4. Horror / Supernatural:
    • Another ‐ A cursed classroom with mysterious deaths.
    • The Promised Neverland ‐ A dark secret behind an orphanage.
    • Parasyte: The Maxim ‐ Alien parasites taking over humans.

  5. Movies:
    1. Your Name (Kimi no Na wa) ‐ A breathtaking romance with time travel elements.
    2. 5 Centimeters Per Second ‐ A realistic and heartbreaking love story about distance and time.
    3. Grave of the Fireflies ‐ A deeply emotional war drama about survival and loss.
    4. A Silent Voice (Koe no Katachi) ‐ A story about redemption, bullying, and friendship.
    5. I Want to Eat Your Pancreas ‐ A heart-wrenching story about love and life.
    6. Paprika ‐ The inspiration behind Inception, with mind-bending dreams.
    7. Demon Slayer: Mugen Train ‐ A breathtaking action-packed adventure.
    8. Jujutsu Kaisen 0 ‐ A prequel to the JJK series with epic battles.
